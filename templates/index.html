How it works

Three steps to safety.

One Tap. Instant Alert.

Press SOS once. No menus, no confirmation. RAKSHAK fires in under a second — GPS captured, contacts notified.

02
GPS. Captured Silently.

Your exact coordinates are captured and encrypted the moment SOS triggers. No manual input needed.

Features

Everything you need to stay safe.

Six powerful modules working together, around the clock.

SOS Emergency

One-tap emergency trigger with instant GPS capture and encrypted broadcast to your trusted contacts.

AI Threat Engine

Silent 2-minute heartbeat pings detect inactivity. Missed pings auto-deploy SOS after 6 minutes.

Danger Zone Map

Community-sourced danger zones on a live map. Entering a hot-zone triggers automatic proximity alerts.

Admin Dashboard

Command center with live tracking, risk scores, user management, and crisis analytics for admins.

Evidence Vault

Tamper-proof incident reports with GPS data, timestamps, and contact logs — exportable as court-ready PDFs.

Secure Auth

Google OAuth + bcrypt hashing, CSRF protection, and rate limiting. Your data stays yours.
